===========
ABOUT Theme
=========== 
The Setmore Spa Salon is a perfect theme for small businesses of different kinds, specifically for Spas and Salons.

The theme is extremely simple to install and customize. With an array of built in menus, templates and graphics, the theme is the perfect embodiment of your business website requisite.

Further, it is compatible to fit the screens of your mobile devices as well. Garner the power of Setmore within a beautiful theme all set to escalate your business goals.

Give your business a new take on appointment scheduling paired with a brilliant website. It’s free, simple and powerful.

This theme has been tested with version 4.0 and above.

Our Demo Site: http://www.setmore.com/demo/

Supported Browsers: We have tested the theme with Firefox, Opera, Chrome and Safari. 

Compatibility with Internet Explorer is unknown.

For any help or support, feel free to contact as at help@setmore.com
